## What is Sound Siphon for Mac?

Sound Siphon for Mac is a **professional audio routing and recording tool** designed to capture sound from any application, device, or input on your macOS system. It enables users to redirect audio between apps, record system audio, and mix multiple sources in real-time—all without additional hardware.

Whether you're streaming, podcasting, recording music, or conducting online meetings, Sound Siphon provides a seamless way to manage your audio workflow. It supports high-quality formats and offers low-latency performance, making it a favorite among broadcasters, musicians, and educators.

The software integrates natively with macOS and works flawlessly on **MacBook Air, MacBook Pro, iMac, and Mac Studio**, including Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3) and Intel-based machines. It is optimized for **macOS Sonoma, Ventura, Monterey, and older versions**, ensuring stability and efficiency.

Key features include:
- Capture audio from any app or browser tab
- Create virtual audio devices for flexible routing
- Record system sound and microphone simultaneously
- Low CPU usage even during long sessions
- Compatible with OBS, Zoom, Logic Pro, and more
